Steal Like an Artist by Austin Kleon

无声对话

Steal Like an Artist: 10 Things Nobody Told You About Being Creative

I’m not an artist, but I’m crazy about creating things, like wording games, planning projects and daydreaming, lol. This book is interesting and easy to read, a small book with a big heart. The author, Austin Kleon, looks very funny and enthusiastic. That is the person who could make complicated things easily accepted and interesting.

Austin Kleon /BY Austin Kleon / CC BY-NC-ND

Chapter 1 Steal Like an Artist

You’re only going to be as good as the stuff you surround yourself with.
…chew on one thinker—writer, artist, activist, role model—you really love.Study everything there is to know about that thinker. Then find three people that thinker loved, and find out everything about them.
“It is better to take what does not belong to you than to let it lie around neglected.” —Mark Twain

Chapter 2 Don’t Wait Until You Know Who You Are to Get Started

You are ready. Start making stuff.

Do it now. Do not wait.

Fake it ‘til you make it.

“If you have one person you’re influenced by, everyone will say you’re the next whoever. But if you rip off a hundred people, everyone will say you’re so original!”
“It is our failure to become our perceived ideal that ultimately defines us and makes us unique.”

Chapter 3 Wirte the Book You Want to Read

Draw the art you want to see, start the business you want to run, play the music you want to hear, write the books you want to read, build the products you want to use—do the work you want to see done.

Chapter 4 Use Your Hands

Step away from the screen.

Use my hands to help me be Creative.

Chapter 5 Side Projects and Hobbies are Important

Everything is connected, in some way.

Chapter 6 The Secret: Do Good Work and Share It with People

Enjoy your obscurity while it lasts. Use it.

Chapter 7 Geography is No longer Our Master

…because of the internet and social media.

Chapter 8 Be Nice

Make friends, ignore enemies.
Stand next to the talent.
You will need:

  1. CURIOSITY
  2. KINDNESS
  3. STAMINA
  4. A WILLINGNESS TO LOOK STUPID

If you ever find that you’re the most talented person in the room, you need to find another room.

Complain is useless, which reminds me the quote from Linus:

Talk is cheap. Show me the code.

Chapter 9 Be Boring.(It’s the only way to get work done)

So happy to see this saying, as boring person.

Chapter 10 Creativity is Subtraction.

The right constraints can lead to your very best work.
“What we respond to in any work of art is the artist’s struggle against his or her limitations.”
What makes us interesting isn’t just what we’ve experienced, but also what we haven’t experienced.

In the end, creativity isn’t just the things we choose to put in, it’s the things we choose to leave out.

陈丹青《退步集》
